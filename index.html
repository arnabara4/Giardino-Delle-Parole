About US
Welcome to the beautiful garden where your words are given flavour by our masterful chefs.Our restaurant was founded by Yukari Yukino in 1978. Eating ambience and customer satisfaction are core values aloong with sustainabale managment of food and environment
The name of the restaurant translates to Garden of Words which is named so by our founder based upon her experience of unrequited love in japan. Their exchange of words in a beautiful garden of shinjuku saved both of them during a critical stage of life and lead to the name of the restaurant
